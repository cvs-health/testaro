Page with deviant menu navigation

This page contains a menu bar. In it, the personalities submenu implements pseudofocus management and the technologies menu implements true focus management. These two patterns are recommended by the Accessible Rich Internet Applications Working Group.

However, the menus manage focus in nonstandard manners.

Choose a domain to toggle that domain’s submenu open or closed. The submenu contains links to definitions (in a new tab).
